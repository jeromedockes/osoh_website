About

The goal of Open Science Office Hours (OSOH) is to integrate students and researchers into the open science community so they feel empowered to do open science

We plan to do this by

  •   Sharing resources on different topics relating to open science
  •   Offering individual support during office hours
  •   Facilitating community discussions on each topic.

We will start by engaging the neuroscience community at McGill University, and perhaps increase the scope in the future.

Open science resources

One of our goals is to help researchers navigate the open-science resources that are available to them. To this end, we have created a page for searching and filtering through resources, according to your needs.
